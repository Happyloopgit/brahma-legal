Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2024

Happyloop Apps ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Brahma mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address (when you sign in with Google)
  • Display name (optional, from Google Sign-In)

User Content:

  • Practice notes you write
  • Custom practice configurations

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Location Data:

  • Approximate location to calculate sunrise times for your Brahmamuhurta window
  • Location is processed locally and not continuously tracked

Device Information:

  • Device type, OS version, app version, timezone

Usage Data:

  • Practice completion data, streaks, app interactions

1.3 Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: Email and display name
  • Firebase: Authentication, analytics, crash reports
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate your personalized Brahmamuhurta window
  • Schedule wake-up alarms
  • Track practice progress and sync across devices
  • Process subscriptions
  • Improve app features and fix bugs

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Settings and practice history stored on device
  • Cloud Storage: Data syncs to Firebase when signed in
  • All transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • We do not store passwords

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase, RevenueCat (with their own privacy policies)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law

5. Your Rights

Account Deletion

Delete your account anytime: Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your data.

Permissions

  • You can deny location permission and manually enter your city
  • You can disable notifications (alarms won't work)

Data Export

Contact happyloopmail@gmail.com to request your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Brahma is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. International Users

Data may be processed in the US (Google, RevenueCat servers). EEA users have GDPR rights including access, rectification, and erasure.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted with an updated date.
