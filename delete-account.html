Delete Your Account

You can delete your Brahma account and all associated data at any time. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

1Open Brahma app

Launch the Brahma app on your device

2Go to Settings

Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the navigation

3Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll down to find the Delete Account option

4Confirm deletion

Read the confirmation message and tap "Delete" to confirm

What Data is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your email address and profile information
  • Practice history and completion data
  • Practice notes you've written
  • Streak and progress data
  • App settings and preferences

What Happens to Your Subscription

Important: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. To avoid future charges, please cancel your subscription separately through:
  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Android: Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Your personal data is deleted immediately from our active systems
  • Backup systems may retain data for up to 30 days before automatic deletion
  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics data (not linked to you) may be retained
