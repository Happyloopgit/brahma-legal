Frequently Asked Questions

What is Brahmamuhurta?

Brahmamuhurta is the "Creator's Hour" - a sacred 48-minute period that begins 1 hour 36 minutes before sunrise. Ancient wisdom considers this the most auspicious time for spiritual practice, meditation, and self-improvement.

Why does the app need my location?

We use your location to calculate the exact sunrise time at your position, which determines your personalized Brahmamuhurta window. The timing varies by location and changes daily throughout the year.

My alarm didn't go off. What happened?

Alarm delivery depends on your device settings. Check:

  • Notification permissions are enabled for Brahma
  • Battery optimization is disabled for the app
  • Do Not Disturb is not blocking notifications
  • The app is not force-closed
How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store:

  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Android: Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions
How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data from our servers.

Can I use Brahma offline?

Yes! Once you've set your location, the app calculates Brahmamuhurta times locally. Internet is only needed for account sync and subscription management.
